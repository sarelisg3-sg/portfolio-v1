## The project

Artium Online is an online music education platform for Spanish-speaking communities. The project involved designing the user experience and visual interface of the platform.

## The approach

The design aimed to communicate the value of music education in an accessible and motivating way — an interface that reflects the seriousness of artistic training without losing warmth.

The site is live in production.
