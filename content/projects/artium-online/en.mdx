## The project

Artium Online is an online music education platform for Spanish-speaking communities. It brings together video lessons for various instruments, support materials, and custom courses — serving also as a pedagogical complement for group and individual lessons taught by professional musicians.

I was the **sole designer** on the project, working alongside a team of professional musicians and music education experts, and the sales team.

## My role

The project spanned multiple design disciplines:

- **Brand identity** — logo design and logo animation
- **Web design** — built on Wix with a custom visual style defined from adapted components; over **60 desktop screens** with full interaction
- **Editorial design** — manuals and support materials for the courses
- **Production** — video upload, button configuration, and full navigation flow setup

## Competitive analysis

A competitive analysis was conducted focused on pricing, business model, and product/service positioning in the online music education market, guiding visual positioning and value communication decisions.

## Results

The site has been live since launch with the following indicators:

- **679 users** on the platform since launch
- **165 monthly sessions**
- Primary audience in **Mexico and the United States**, with presence in the UAE, Colombia, and Spain
